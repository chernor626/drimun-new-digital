Peacebuilding & SDGs

Transforming young peacebuilders into advocates for social cohesion, climate action, and the Sustainable Development Goals across African communities.

Programme overview

From dialogue to action

Peace is not simply the absence of conflict — it is the presence of justice, inclusion, and opportunity. Our Peacebuilding & SDGs programme equips young Africans to be active agents of peace and sustainable development in their communities.

Participants learn how policy dialogue, community organising, and evidence‑based advocacy can amplify youth voices in decision‑making spaces. They engage directly with the 17 Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), exploring how local action drives global progress.

This programme blends workshops, simulations, field projects, and mentorship to create a holistic learning experience that turns passion into measurable impact.
