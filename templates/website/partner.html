Partner with Us

Join forces with Dream MUN to amplify youth voices, build leadership pipelines, and drive sustainable development across Africa.

Why partner

Together we go further

When organisations invest in youth diplomacy, they help build a generation of skilled, ethical, and globally minded leaders. Partnership with Dream MUN offers tangible benefits for your organisation and the continent.

🎯

Brand Visibility

Your logo and name featured across our conferences, digital platforms, and communications – reaching thousands of young people, educators, and policy makers.

🤝

Talent Pipeline

Direct access to trained, motivated youth ready for internships, scholarships, and early‑career opportunities in diplomacy, policy, and development.

🌍

SDG Impact

Align your CSR or institutional goals with measurable contributions to SDG 4 (Quality Education), SDG 5 (Gender Equality), and SDG 16 (Peace, Justice & Strong Institutions).

Ways to collaborate

Partnership models

🏛️

Institutional Partner

Universities, schools, and government bodies can co‑host conferences, provide venues, or integrate Dream MUN into academic programmes.

💼

Corporate Sponsor

Sponsor a conference, a scholarship fund, or a specific programme track. Receive brand exposure, delegate engagement, and impact reporting.

🌱

NGO / Civil Society

Collaborate on peacebuilding, SDG advocacy, and community projects. Share expertise and co‑design workshops that align with your mission.

📣

Media Partner

Amplify youth voices through coverage, storytelling, and digital campaigns. Help us reach new audiences across the continent.
