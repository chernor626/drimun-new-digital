About Dream MUN

A platform created to help young people move from potential to presence in diplomacy, leadership, peacebuilding and global governance.

5+Years of Excellence
Our identity

We prepare young Africans for rooms where decisions are made.

Dream MUN Sierra Leone exists to give youth practical exposure to diplomacy, policy writing, public speaking, international relations and ethical leadership. The organization turns complex global issues into structured learning experiences where young people can debate, negotiate and design solutions.

Our approach combines Model United Nations simulations, innovation training, SDG advocacy, peacebuilding dialogue, diplomatic protocol and mentorship. This creates a bridge between education, civic participation and professional opportunity.
