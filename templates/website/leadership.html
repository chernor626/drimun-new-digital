Youth Leadership & Innovation

Equipping young Africans with the mindset, skills, and confidence to lead ethically and innovate boldly in their communities and beyond.

Programme overview

Beyond the conference room

Leadership doesn't happen by accident — it's built through practice, reflection, and exposure to real‑world challenges. Our Youth Leadership & Innovation programme is designed to turn passionate young people into effective, values‑driven leaders.

Through interactive workshops, design‑thinking labs, public communication coaching, and team‑based projects, participants develop the competencies that matter in diplomacy, governance, business, and civil society.

This programme runs alongside our core MUN activities and is open to all registered delegates who want to deepen their leadership journey.
